Filmmaking is a big hobby of mine; I'm most interested in cinematography and editing, where all the real magic happens. These are some of my favorite projects.


Yamatai

Yamatai Promo, 2015

This project was all about capture a glimpse of what its like to be behind the drum. The sountrack for the video was entirely composed/arranged/performed by my myself and our musical director, Tyler Angulo. Role: Write, Direct, Film, Edit

Yamatai Promo, 2014

The taiko drummers of Cornell are being summoned for the concert of the year. This promo easily had the most story-telling worked into it and was a lot of fun (and very cold!) to make. Role: Write, Direct, Film, Edit

Yamatai Promo, 2013

An epic build up showcasing the work ethic and comradery that runs deep in our club to master the japanese art form that is Taiko. Got to learn some japenese words and work in some cool slow-mo video shot on a Phantom. Role: Write, Direct, Film, Edit

Yamatai Promo, 2012

The goal for this promo was to frame the grandioseness of taiko against the very fitting backdrop of Cornell. This was the most popular promo video we made, and was even featured on Cornell's home page for a week! Role: Write, Direct, Film, Edit
